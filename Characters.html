Characters

Anthony John Soprano "Tony"

Is a fictional character and the antihero protagonist in the HBO television drama series The Sopranos portrayed by James Gandolfini.

Christopher Moltisanti

Easily the show’s most tragic character, Christopher was a lost soul from the start. Without a proper father figure and an alcoholic mother, Chris spent his entire life around the mob and, unlike Tony.

Carmela Soprano

While Dr. Melfi serves as a moral compass, Carmela represents what it’s like to struggle with the idea of having one.

Dr. Jennifer Melfi

No other character better encompasses what it’s like to have a moral compass than Dr. Melfi. She tried her best time and time again to steer Tony in the right direction with sound advice.

AJ Soprano

AJ gets the nod over Meadow (first daugther of Tony) for his more complex characteristics. While seen as a spoiled brat well, he is and an overall narcissist too caught up in his own self-worth to think of anyone but himself.

Paulie Gualtieri

One of the longest and loyal members of Tony’s crew, Paulie at first appeared to be nothing more than a common mercenary, running “errands” and collecting payments.
